## CASE STUDIES

### Boldin — Withdrawal Order
I designed a personalized withdrawal strategy feature for Boldin's retirement platform. The problem: retirees had no way to control how their savings were drawn down to minimize taxes and extend longevity. My solution combined three modes — the Dynamic Solver (AI-powered optimization), the Explorer (side-by-side strategy comparison), and manual drag-and-drop customization — giving users transparency and control over their financial future. The result: 40% user engagement, 18% upgrade rate, and 10% reduction in monthly cancellations.

### Boldin — Design System
I built Boldin's scalable semantic design system from the ground up — spanning typography, components, tokens, and interaction patterns across both B2C and enterprise B2B products. The system had to flex across white-label partners including Mercer, RTX, American Fidelity, and Nationwide, while preserving Boldin's core brand DNA. The Nationwide integration alone supported 32,000+ plan users and replaced legacy infrastructure.

### Boldin — Brand & Social
I led the 2024 rebrand from NewRetirement to Boldin. This meant creating a full brand identity from scratch — logo, typography, color palette, iconography — centered on confidence and financial empowerment. I also developed a warm illustration style blending hand-crafted and AI-assisted work, and ran paid social campaigns that broke the mold of typical fintech with bold creative and human storytelling.

### BlockFi
I redesigned BlockFi's onboarding and KYC verification flows to dramatically reduce friction. The original experience had 20+ screens. My redesign streamlined it into a personalized, intelligent flow by integrating Telesign, LexisNexis, and Sentilink for smarter identity verification. Results: 66% fewer pages and fields, 10%+ conversion lift, $1.5M in new revenue, and $76K in operational savings from reduced manual review.

### JetBlue
I redesigned JetBlue's end-to-end flight booking experience across five parallel initiatives: flight results, shopping cart, checkout, seat selection, and payment. I ran 12 qualitative and 50 quantitative tests to validate key UX decisions — including filter navigation over accordion patterns — enabling users to compare fare classes side by side. The work increased booking conversion and reduced post-search drop-off.

### UBS
I redesigned UBS Wealthway, a wealth management iPad app for high-net-worth individuals. The shift was from transactional dashboards to conversational, human-centered planning. My central concept was a "cube" representing six dimensions of wealth: net worth, cashflow, milestones, relationships, interests, and advice. Features included goal-setting tools, milestone timelines, and asset visualization. User testing showed 94% perceived ease-of-use and 76% indicated download intent.

### Kidventure Hub
I built an MVP playground-finder guide for NYC parents — solving the problem that most parenting listicles are outdated and full of broken links. The brand uses neo-brutalist design to convey confidence and community. I used Supabase for the backend, Figma tokens for theming, and a vibe-coded design-to-dev workflow with Loveable.dev. The app includes 100+ curated spots with quick-scan cards showing essentials: bathrooms, amenities, nearby food — all in a mobile-first responsive design.
